# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in MrNothing Shield a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

### Positive Behavior
- Using respectful and inclusive language
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and end users
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Prioritizing user safety and privacy in all decisions

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Promotion or development of offensive security tools (spyware, malware)
- Sharing private information without consent
- Harassment, trolling, or inflammatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations may result in temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

## Scope

This code applies within project spaces and when representing the project publicly.
